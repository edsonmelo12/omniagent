# Content Plan

Content plan for Amiclube's organic social cycle.

## Objectives

1. Increase qualified reach among artisans and women entrepreneurs in the amigurumi niche.
   - KPI: follower growth, reach, profile visits.
   - Target: steady week-over-week growth, not viral spikes.

2. Build authority around pricing, selling and organizing handmade offers.
   - KPI: saves, shares, comments, story replies.
   - Target: consistent engagement on educational posts.

3. Turn the catalog and educational content into a repeatable organic system.
   - KPI: content output consistency, completion rate on video, carousel engagement.
   - Target: a reliable weekly publishing rhythm.

## Content Pillars

- Pricing and profitability
  - Purpose: help artisans understand how to price and protect margin.
  - Example topics: how to calculate a product price, why low pricing hurts the business, pricing mistakes.

- Selling and positioning
  - Purpose: show how artisans can present offers more clearly and convert better.
  - Example topics: how to sell amigurumi on Instagram and Facebook, how to present a product offer, how to improve product pages and captions.

- Organization and routine
  - Purpose: reduce chaos and make the business feel manageable.
  - Example topics: content routine for artisans, how to organize the catalog, weekly planning for handmade sales.

- Proof and process
  - Purpose: increase trust and reduce buyer uncertainty.
  - Example topics: behind the scenes, product making, before/after organization, customer outcomes, practical demonstrations.

- Education for artisans
  - Purpose: turn Amiclube into a useful reference, not only a store or catalog.
  - Example topics: marketing basics for artisans, how to grow a handmade profile, how to use content to support sales.

## Platform Roles

- Instagram Feed: educational carousels, proof posts and product storytelling.
- Instagram Reels: discovery, quick tips and practical demonstrations.
- Instagram Stories: relationship, polls, quick FAQs and daily trust building.
- Facebook: community content, longer explanations, social proof and direct conversation.
- TikTok: fast discovery content, practical hooks and short educational videos.
- Pinterest: evergreen discovery for products, tutorials and visual inspiration.

## Cadence

- Instagram Feed: 2 carousels per week.
- Instagram Reels: 2 videos per week.
- Instagram Stories: 3 blocks per week.
- Facebook: 2 posts per week, adapted from the strongest educational angles.
- TikTok: 2 short videos per week, focused on discovery and practical advice.
- Pinterest: 3 to 5 pins per week, repurposed from carousels, products and visual tips.

## Measurement

- Reach and impressions: reviewed weekly from platform insights.
- Follower growth: reviewed weekly to spot momentum or stagnation.
- Saves and shares: reviewed weekly as the main signal of content value.
- Comments and replies: reviewed weekly to understand resonance.
- Profile visits and link clicks: reviewed weekly for commercial intent.
- Video retention and completion: reviewed weekly on Reels and TikTok.
- Content consistency: reviewed biweekly to keep the cadence realistic.
