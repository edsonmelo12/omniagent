# Market Intel

Real Engenharia market synthesis for the current organic social cycle.

## Executive Summary

- Real Engenharia should be treated as a portfolio of distinct residential products, not one generic real estate brand.
- Each empreendimento has a different buyer motivation, stage of decision and content angle.
- The strongest organic opportunity is to connect project stage, lifestyle benefit and buyer fit.
- Instagram and Facebook should work as the main discovery and trust channels.
- The content system should help people self-select the right empreendimento faster.

## Audience

- Primary segment: active home buyers comparing residential options in Belém.
- Secondary segment: families, investors and upgraders evaluating launch, ready and completed properties.
- Main pains: uncertainty about price-value fit, construction stage, location tradeoffs and confidence in delivery.
- Content habits: visual tours, location cues, progress updates, lifestyle benefits and proof of seriousness.

## Competitive Landscape

- Many real estate brands communicate at the company level but fail to separate each project clearly.
- Generic property marketing often emphasizes aesthetics without helping the buyer understand fit.
- Real Engenharia can differentiate by giving each empreendimento its own narrative, audience and proof points.

## Opportunities

1. Build separate organic narratives for Real Towers, Real Liberty, Real Paradise and Porto Real.
2. Use project stage as a content theme: launch, ready, completed and near completion.
3. Translate each empreendimento into buyer-specific benefits, not just feature lists.
4. Create comparison-friendly content that helps buyers self-select faster.
5. Reuse institutional credibility to support confidence at the project level.

## Risks and Assumptions

- Assumption: buyers respond better to clarity and fit than to broad institutional messaging.
- Risk: if all empreendimentos are pushed with the same voice, the message loses relevance.
- Risk: the content team may need separate calendars or series per project.
- Risk: social links and project landing links should be confirmed before future campaigns rely on them.
