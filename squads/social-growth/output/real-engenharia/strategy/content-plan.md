# Content Plan

Content plan for Real Engenharia's organic social cycle.

## Objectives

1. Clarify the role of each empreendimento in the portfolio.
   - KPI: saves, shares, profile visits.
   - Target: more qualified interest in the right project.

2. Increase trust around stage, location and delivery confidence.
   - KPI: comments, replies, direct messages, link clicks.
   - Target: stronger buyer confidence per project.

3. Turn the portfolio into a repeatable content system by project.
   - KPI: content consistency, video completion, engagement rate.
   - Target: a reliable weekly rhythm by empreendimento.

## Content Pillars

- Institutional trust
  - Purpose: reinforce brand credibility and seriousness.
  - Example topics: who Real Engenharia is, why the market trusts the brand, what the portfolio says about the company.

- Premium desire
  - Purpose: make the high-end offer feel aspirational and exclusive.
  - Example topics: Real Towers, front to the bay, lifestyle, status, design and location.

- Ready-to-move confidence
  - Purpose: support buyers who want convenience and fast decision-making.
  - Example topics: Real Liberty, move-in readiness, practical benefits, time savings, certainty.

- Delivery and security
  - Purpose: reduce risk perception and increase trust.
  - Example topics: Real Paradise, completed work, confidence in delivery, what completion means for the buyer.

- Entry and practicality
  - Purpose: make the more accessible project easy to understand and compare.
  - Example topics: Porto Real, functional living, value, practicality, first purchase or upgrade.

## Platform Roles

- Instagram Feed: carousels and image-led posts for project storytelling and comparison.
- Instagram Reels: short tours, quick project highlights and visual proof.
- Instagram Stories: Q&A, polls and qualification questions.
- Facebook: longer explanations, location context and broader local reach.

## Cadence

- Instagram Feed: 2 posts per week.
- Instagram Reels: 2 videos per week.
- Instagram Stories: 3 blocks per week.
- Facebook: 2 posts per week.

## Measurement

- Reach and impressions: reviewed weekly from platform insights.
- Profile visits and link clicks: reviewed weekly for project interest.
- Saves and shares: reviewed weekly as the strongest value signal.
- Comments and direct messages: reviewed weekly to understand buyer intent.
- Video retention: reviewed weekly on Reels.
- Content consistency: reviewed biweekly to keep the cadence realistic.
