# Creative DNA - Real Engenharia

## Scope

Client: Real Engenharia
Use this DNA as the default creative frame for the brand-level portfolio communication.

## Brand Reading

- Segment: real estate / residential portfolio
- Tone: sophisticated, confident and clear
- Core promise: help the buyer understand which project fits their moment
- Brand behavior: premium, segmented, authoritative, aspirational

## Creative DNA

- Brand feel: premium, controlled and high trust
- Mood: editorial, elegant and decisive
- Energy level: medium, restrained
- Creative role: authority plus conversion
- Visual identity: segmented by empreendimento, never generic

## Palette Anchors

- Base: deep charcoal, navy, graphite, white and warm neutral
- Accents: gold, silver or refined light neutrals where needed
- Rule: keep contrast elegant and premium

## Image Strategy

- Preferred image story: architecture, facade, interiors, location, skyline, lifestyle and project stage
- Preferred image types: full-bleed architecture, premium background image, clean project framing, location-led support
- Best use of background image: full-bleed premium backdrop with a controlled overlay or clean editorial space when the message needs room
- Anti-pattern: generic real estate brochure visuals

## Background Treatment

- Prefer wide compositions with depth and clear focal areas
- Use dark overlays for premium feel when the image carries the mood
- Preserve the architectural line and the project silhouette
- Avoid decorative clutter and weak contrast

## Typography Direction

- Premium headline behavior
- Clean sans body with possible serif-led emphasis on titles
- Strong spacing and hierarchy
- Short, assertive copy
- Avoid promotional clutter and crowded layouts

## Composition Family

- Editorial
- Proof-led
- Comparison
- Manifesto
- Closing

## CTA Style

- Prefer compare, know more, visit, save or contact CTAs
- Keep the CTA elegant and direct
- Avoid aggressive or casual calls to action

## Do

- Keep every empreendimento treated as its own story
- Use a premium visual voice across the portfolio
- Preserve brand authority with clear structure
- Make the buyer self-select faster
- Let the image do the heavy lifting when the project already has strong visual proof

## Don't

- Do not blend all empreendimentos into one visual language
- Do not use playful or craft-like language
- Do not overdecorate the frame
- Do not turn the brand into a template brochure
- Do not lose the premium tone

## Batch Guardrails

- One batch can be premium, but the internal logic must still vary by empreendimentos
- Compare, proof and close should be rotated across assets
- When the theme is image-led, use atmosphere first and text second
- Keep the social voice aligned to the commercial role of each project
