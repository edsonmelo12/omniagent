# Content Plan

Content plan for Porto Real's organic social cycle.

## Objectives

1. Make the value of Porto Real instantly understandable.
   - KPI: saves, profile visits, direct messages.
   - Target: more qualified interest from practical buyers.

2. Increase trust around layout, stage and location.
   - KPI: comments, replies, link clicks.
   - Target: clearer intent and fewer objections.

3. Turn practicality into the main organic narrative.
   - KPI: engagement rate, content consistency, video completion.
   - Target: a repeatable weekly rhythm around the product.

## Content Pillars

- Functional living
  - Purpose: show how the project fits real life.
  - Example topics: 2 and 3 bedroom layouts, garage, everyday convenience.

- Cost-benefit
  - Purpose: make the offer feel smart, not just affordable.
  - Example topics: what the buyer gets, why the value makes sense, how to compare options.

- Stage and confidence
  - Purpose: reduce uncertainty about the project.
  - Example topics: phase final de acabamento, what that means, why it matters.

- Location and access
  - Purpose: connect the project to daily routine and neighborhood context.
  - Example topics: Pedreira, access, proximity, convenience and urban living.

- Buyer clarity
  - Purpose: help people self-select if Porto Real is for them.
  - Example topics: who should consider the project, what kind of buyer it serves, comparison posts.

## Platform Roles

- Instagram Feed: carousels with comparisons and practical explanations.
- Instagram Reels: quick explanations, location cues and visual proof.
- Instagram Stories: qualification questions and responses.
- Facebook: context-rich posts for broader local audience reach.

## Cadence

- Instagram Feed: 2 posts per week.
- Instagram Reels: 2 videos per week.
- Instagram Stories: 3 blocks per week.
- Facebook: 2 posts per week.

## Measurement

- Reach and impressions: reviewed weekly from platform insights.
- Saves and shares: reviewed weekly as the main value signal.
- Profile visits and direct messages: reviewed weekly for intent.
- Comments and replies: reviewed weekly to identify objections.
- Video retention: reviewed weekly on Reels.
- Content consistency: reviewed biweekly to keep the cadence realistic.
