# Health Summary

## Executive Summary

- Porto Real should be monitored as a conversion-friendly entry product, not as a premium desire play.
- The main signal to watch is whether the content helps buyers understand value, practicality and decision clarity.
- Instagram and Facebook are the key channels for trust and intent.
- If saves, profile visits and direct messages increase, the message is landing.
- If reach rises but intent stays low, the content needs a sharper value proposition.

## Priority KPIs

- profile visits
- direct messages
- saves and shares
- comments and replies
- reach per post
- engagement rate
- video completion rate
- link clicks

## Signal Reading

- High reach with low intent: the content is visible, but not specific enough.
- Good saves and shares with low profile visits: the post is helpful, but not tied tightly enough to the offer.
- Strong comments about price or size: the audience is comparing, which can be used to qualify interest.
- Low video completion: the hook or pace needs to improve.

## Recommended Actions

1. Reinforce practical benefits in hooks and captions.
2. Use more comparison posts that explain why Porto Real makes sense.
3. Simplify CTA language and point to one next step.
4. Add more visual proof of layout, location and project stage.
5. If intent remains low after two cycles, shift the focus from general awareness to sharper buyer qualification.

## Monitoring Rhythm

- Weekly: review best and worst posts.
- Fortnightly: compare reach, saves, profile visits and DMs.
- Monthly: decide whether to keep Porto Real as a standalone cycle or fold it into a broader portfolio cycle.

## Notes

- This summary should be read together with the approved content plan and schedule plan.
- If the commercial priority changes, update the brief before the next review.
