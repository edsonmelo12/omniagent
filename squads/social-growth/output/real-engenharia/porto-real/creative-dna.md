# Creative DNA - Porto Real

## Scope

Client: Porto Real
Subworkspace: Real Engenharia portfolio
Use this DNA as the local creative frame for the practical and entry-friendly project narrative.

## Brand Reading

- Segment: accessible residential option inside the Real Engenharia portfolio
- Tone: clear, practical and confident
- Core promise: help the buyer understand value, function and decision fit
- Brand behavior: less premium than the parent portfolio, more direct and utilitarian

## Creative DNA

- Brand feel: practical, reliable and buyer-friendly
- Mood: clear, calm and decision-oriented
- Energy level: medium-low to medium
- Creative role: clarity plus trust
- Visual identity: functional, simple and comparison-friendly

## Palette Anchors

- Base: neutral light background, balanced dark contrast, simple color system
- Accents: restrained blue, graphite, charcoal or brand-safe neutral accents
- Rule: avoid premium excess; keep the palette approachable

## Image Strategy

- Preferred image story: home use, layout, practicality, accessibility, location, stage and everyday benefits
- Preferred image types: clean project photo, layout support, comparison frame, practical benefit card
- Best use of background image: clear support image or architectural frame with light-to-medium overlay
- Anti-pattern: luxury-only imagery that mispositions the offer

## Background Treatment

- Prefer clean, readable compositions
- Use overlay only as much as needed for contrast
- Keep the background honest and functional
- Avoid overdramatic premium treatment

## Typography Direction

- Straightforward headline hierarchy
- Readable sans pairings
- Simple structure and quick scanning
- Numbers, dimensions and functional cues should be easy to read
- Avoid ornate type or over-designed posters

## Composition Family

- Educational
- Comparison
- Editorial
- Proof-led
- Closing

## CTA Style

- Prefer save, compare, ask, know more or contact CTAs
- Keep the CTA objective and low-friction
- Make the next step obvious

## Do

- Highlight function, location and value
- Explain what the project solves
- Use comparison to reduce decision friction
- Keep the visual language accessible and trustworthy
- Make the copy feel calm and useful

## Don't

- Do not use the same voice as the premium projects
- Do not overpromise luxury
- Do not overdecorate the frame
- Do not make the content feel expensive or distant
- Do not bury the practical data

## Batch Guardrails

- The project should feel like a smart choice, not a prestige play
- Use clarity over drama
- Comparison and practicality should appear early in the batch
- Keep the visuals steady, calm and highly legible

## Source Signals

- Public market intel: practical, accessible and decision-friendly position
- Core audience: first-time buyers and practical upgraders
- Content focus: 2 and 3 bedrooms, 1-car garage, square meters and final finishing stage
