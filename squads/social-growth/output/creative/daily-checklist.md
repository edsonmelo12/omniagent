# Daily Checklist

Client: active client
Cycle: current cycle

## Before Starting

- Confirm the title of the day.
- Confirm the primary asset type.
- Confirm whether the product image is required.
- Confirm the channel adaptations for Instagram, Facebook and Pinterest.
- Confirm the CTA and the visual hierarchy.
- Confirm the batch passes the [Creative Validation Checklist](validation-checklist.md).
- Confirm the source class if the day needs external imagery.
- Confirm the free/public source search note, candidate types and selection rationale if external imagery is used.

## Build Checklist

- Build the Instagram master asset first.
- Keep the cover readable in one glance on mobile.
- Keep the hook short and the contrast high.
- Keep body text light and structured.
- Keep the CTA isolated and obvious.

## Product Image Rules

- Use a product image only when it strengthens proof, trust or value.
- Use a process image when the post depends on bastidor or evidence.
- Do not force product imagery into clarity or thesis posts.
- Do not use AI image generation on the first pass.
- Use the source-decision matrix to choose the least risky source class.
- Keep paid stock out of the default path.

## Channel Adaptation Checklist

### Instagram
- Master version is complete.
- Hook is visible immediately.
- Layout is native to the format.

### Facebook
- Caption is shortened.
- Layout density is reduced.
- Strongest frame is reused when useful.

### Pinterest
- Composition is vertical.
- Title is the first thing the eye reads.
- The pin can be saved without losing context.

### Stories
- The hook is repeated in a simpler form.
- Proof or context is shown in one frame.
- Interaction cue or CTA is present.

## Review Checklist

- Mobile readability passes.
- CTA is clear and actionable.
- Product use is justified.
- Visual system matches the approved direction.
- The post still feels like the client, not a generic template.
- The creative validation checklist passed before render.
- Any external asset has a documented source class, source search note, license and rationale.

## Publish Checklist

- Final asset exported.
- Files named consistently.
- Channel versions saved.
- Schedule updated.
- Publish only after review is complete.

## Daily Delivery Order

1. Build IG master.
2. Produce proof/product asset if required.
3. Adapt to FB and PIN.
4. Review mobile readability.
5. Approve and queue for publishing.

## Short Reminder

- Keep the message simple.
- Keep the product visible only when it helps.
- Keep the design readable before it is decorative.
