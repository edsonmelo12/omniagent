# Campaign Planning Pack

## Purpose

Capture the evidence needed to plan a new campaign without relying on memory or guesswork.

## Required Inputs

- previous campaign outcomes
- engagement by format, theme and channel
- market context
- trend signals
- audience behavior
- proof gaps
- current client record
- active offer and funnel stage

## Structure

### 1. Campaign History Read

- what worked
- what worked partially
- what failed
- what is still inconclusive

### 2. Engagement Read

- strongest format
- strongest topic
- strongest channel
- best save/share/reply signal
- weakest signal

### 3. Market and Trend Read

- relevant market shifts
- relevant competitor moves
- relevant trend signals
- what is noise

### 4. Proof Read

- proof available
- proof missing
- proof gaps to close

### 5. Decision Portfolio

- `scale`
- `optimize`
- `retest`
- `pause`
- `archive`

### 6. Campaign Thesis

- one thesis
- one primary objection
- one primary proof point
- one primary CTA
- channel role map
- validation metric

### 7. Image Research Follow-up (only if needed)

- if the gate already captured `now/later`, do not repeat it here
- if `later`, what will trigger the image research step
- if `now`, what scope is needed (blog hero, social derivatives, campaign covers, export batch)
- source types planned
- timing by week or by demand

## Output Rule

No strategy brief is valid unless this pack is completed first.
