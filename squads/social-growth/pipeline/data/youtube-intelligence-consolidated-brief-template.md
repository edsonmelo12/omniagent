# YouTube Intelligence Consolidated Brief Template

## Purpose

Template multi-cliente para consolidar leituras de lote de vídeos/podcasts em uma síntese reutilizável pela squad.

## Fixed Fields

- source canal or source list
- analysis window
- objective of the analysis
- central thesis
- dominant patterns
- reusable strategies
- operational rules
- conflict resolution
- agent routing notes
- next action

## Expansion Signals Beyond the Initial Window

- trend intelligence and recurring briefing systems
- IA as a support layer for research and filtering
- pricing, value, scale, acquisition, authority, and proof
- repeated strategy concepts that confirm patterns but do not expand them

## Variable Fields by Client

- client name
- target audience
- active offer
- current objection
- strongest channel
- proof available
- funnel stage priority
- CTA priority
- risk constraints
- metric of validation
