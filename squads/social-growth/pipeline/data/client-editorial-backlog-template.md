# Client Editorial Backlog Template

## Purpose

Keep a persistent per-client backlog of recurring SEO/GEO discrepancies and future editorial improvements.

## Output Structure

```
# Client Editorial Backlog

## Client
- name:
- updated at:

## Recurring Discrepancies
| ID | Type | Severity | Pattern | Impact | Owner | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Future Topic Opportunities
| Topic | Why It Matters | Trigger Signal | Priority | Target Cycle |
|---|---|---|---|---|

## Editorial Decisions Log
| Date | Decision | Why | Revisit Trigger |
|---|---|---|---|

## Performance Learnings
| Insight | Evidence | Action |
|---|---|---|
```

## Usage Rule

After review, append or update this backlog so future cycles reuse the same learning instead of rediscovering it.
