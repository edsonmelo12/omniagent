# Campaign Planning Gate

## Purpose

Make campaign planning mandatory before any new campaign brief or content plan is produced.

## When to use

Use this gate before strategy, whenever the team starts a new campaign, renews a cycle, or considers a major content shift.

## Required Inputs

- previous campaign outcomes
- engagement by format, theme and channel
- what worked
- what failed
- market context
- trend signals
- audience behavior
- proof gaps
- current client record
- active offer and funnel stage
- video intelligence brief or adapted video intelligence brief
- image research decision (`now` or `later`)

## Mandatory Questions

1. What did previous campaigns prove?
2. What should be repeated, optimized, retested or paused?
3. What does engagement say about format, topic and channel?
4. What does the market say right now?
5. What trend or external signal matters enough to influence the cycle?
6. What proof is missing?
7. What is the safest and strongest campaign thesis for this cycle?
8. Which video intelligence insight supports this thesis and decision?
9. Image research selector:
   - `Deseja fazer a pesquisa das imagens agora?`
   - `Sim` -> continue with image sourcing and export planning; record image research mode as `now`.
   - `Não` -> continue with textual planning only; record image research mode as `later`.

## Output

- campaign history read
- engagement read
- market and trend read
- decision portfolio (`scale`, `optimize`, `retest`, `pause`, `archive`)
- one campaign thesis
- one primary objection
- one primary proof point
- one primary CTA
- channel role map
- validation metric
- video intelligence reference chain
- image research selector prompt used
- image research mode (`now` | `later`)

## Veto Conditions

Do not advance to strategy if any of these are true:

- previous campaign evidence was not reviewed
- engagement was ignored
- market context was not checked
- trend signals were not considered when relevant
- proof gaps were not identified
- the campaign thesis is only a topic, not a decision
- video intelligence was not reviewed or adapted
- the image research decision was not recorded

## Output Rule

No new campaign should be planned without this gate completed and recorded.
