# Campaign Review Pack

## Purpose

Close the campaign loop by recording what happened, why it happened, and what must change next.

## Required Inputs

- published or approved assets
- campaign metrics
- engagement by format, theme and channel
- client feedback
- market context
- trend context
- previous planning pack

## Structure

### 1. What Happened

- what was published
- what was approved but not published
- what was published late or differently than planned

### 2. Performance Read

- strongest response
- strongest saves or shares
- strongest replies or visits
- weakest asset
- weakest channel

### 3. Decision Read

- what worked
- what worked partially
- what failed by execution
- what failed by distribution
- what remains inconclusive

### 4. Learning Read

- thesis confirmed
- thesis weakened
- false-negative suspects
- angles to repeat
- angles to stop

### 5. Next-Cycle Seed

- next thesis
- next objection
- next proof gap
- next CTA
- next channel role

## Decision Labels

- `scale`
- `optimize`
- `retest`
- `pause`
- `archive`

## Output Rule

No new planning pack should be created until this review pack is completed and filed.
