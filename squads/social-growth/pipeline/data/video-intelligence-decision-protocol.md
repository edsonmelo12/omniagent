# Video Intelligence Decision Protocol

## Purpose

Make video intelligence a required input for campaign decisions so the squad does not fall back to guesswork.

## When to use

Use this protocol before any new campaign thesis, content plan, CTA decision, or channel allocation is finalized.

## Required Inputs

- consolidated video intelligence brief
- client adaptation layer
- previous campaign evidence
- current client record
- active offer and funnel stage
- proof gaps

## Mandatory Rule

No campaign action is valid unless it can be traced to all three of these layers:

1. video intelligence insight
2. campaign evidence
3. AmiClube adaptation

## Decision Format

Every recommended action must name:

- source insight
- dominant mechanism
- client adaptation
- campaign decision
- validation signal

## Guardrails

- If the insight is missing, stop and request it.
- If the adaptation is generic, refine it before using it.
- If the decision cannot be traced, do not advance it to strategy.
- If the evidence conflicts, prefer the stronger confirmed signal and record the conflict.

## Required Output

- insight used
- mechanism extracted
- what changed because of the insight
- what remains unproven
- what metric will validate the decision

## Quality Check

A campaign recommendation passes only when the reasoning chain is explicit:

`video insight -> campaign evidence -> AmiClube adaptation -> campaign decision -> validation`
