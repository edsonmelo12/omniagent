# Atos Analista - Deliverables

## Purpose

Define the canonical output formats for `Atos Analista`.
Each cadence must have its own deliverable.

## Deliverable 1 - Asset Card

### Audience

Execution team

### Purpose

Provide a fast, decision-ready read of a single asset.

### Required Sections

- `Asset Identification`
- `Client Context`
- `Primary Objective`
- `Return Horizon`
- `Observed Signals`
- `Short Read`
- `Decision`
- `Probable Causality`
- `Confidence`
- `Immediate Next Action`

### Usage Rule

The asset card should be readable in under one minute.
It exists to inform action, not to provide exhaustive explanation.

## Deliverable 2 - Weekly Operational Review

### Audience

Execution team and strategy owner

### Purpose

Turn the week's evidence into execution adjustments.

### Required Sections

- `Executive Summary`
- `Client Scope`
- `Real Wins`
- `Losses / Waste`
- `Emerging Patterns`
- `Conflicts`
- `Themes / Formats / Channels Under Watch`
- `Next Week Backlog`
- `Alerts`

### Usage Rule

The weekly review must always end with a short prioritized backlog.
If it does not change next-week action, it failed.

## Deliverable 3 - Monthly Portfolio Memo

### Audience

Leadership and client-facing strategic readers

### Purpose

Provide a portfolio-level reading of what is strengthening, weakening, saturating or misaligned.

### Required Sections

- `Central Thesis of the Month`
- `Client Scope`
- `Portfolio State by Objective`
- `Portfolio State by Horizon`
- `Strengthened Theses`
- `Weakening Theses`
- `Most Coherent Channels`
- `Least Coherent Channels`
- `Editorial Efficiency`
- `Business Contribution`
- `Saturation Risks`
- `Priority Decisions for the Next Cycle`

### Usage Rule

The monthly memo is the main strategic artifact for `Atos Analista`.
It must read like a decision memo, not like a metric dump.

## Shared Output Rules

Every deliverable must include:
- explicit confidence
- explicit gaps when relevant
- explicit next action or priority consequence
- explicit client context

## Tone Rule

Deliverables should be:
- concise
- analytical
- decision-oriented
- free from decorative jargon

## Destination Mapping

### Team

Needs:
- asset cards
- weekly review
- short backlog

### Leadership

Needs:
- weekly review
- monthly memo
- portfolio and investment reading

### Client

Needs:
- executive summary derived from the monthly memo
- strategic insights
- prioritized changes

## Output Failure Modes

Reject the deliverable if:
- it only lists metrics
- it hides uncertainty
- it has no decision
- it is too long for its cadence
- it does not change what happens next
