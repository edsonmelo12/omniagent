# Blog Brief Template

## Purpose

Create a single canonical brief before architecture and drafting begin.
This file is the bridge between strategy and long-form writing.

## Required Sections

### Topic and Intent
- topic
- primary keyword
- search intent
- target audience
- business goal

### Editorial Thesis
- single-sentence thesis
- why this matters now
- what the article must prove

### Structure Family
- selected family
- why the family fits
- repetition risk to avoid

### Proof Freshness Gate
- proof status: current / historical / partial / missing
- proof recency
- proof sources
- what cannot be claimed yet

### Trend and Validity Gate
- trend source
- signal observation date
- validity window: 30d / 60d / 90d / evergreen
- confidence: high / medium / low
- stale-risk note

### Family Ledger Entry
- family name
- opening shape
- proof placement
- nuance placement
- close shape
- neighboring-post guardrail

### CTA Direction
- primary CTA
- funnel stage
- action expected from the reader

### Copy Brief Seed
- target reader
- reader problem
- awareness level
- promise territory
- dominant objection
- hook territory

### Featured Image Direction
- visual concept
- thesis match
- one-glance job
- source class

### Word Count Target
- tactical / standard / pillar
- rationale

## Usage Rule

The blog architecture step should read this brief before writing the long-form outline.
The discovery optimization step should preserve the thesis and the family-specific arc from this brief.
