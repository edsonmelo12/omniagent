# Architecture Brief

Turn the locked angle into a drafting-grade architecture that preserves creativity and credibility.

## Process

1. Convert the chosen angle into an explicit thesis, copy brief lock, proof map, and family-specific arc.
2. Define the opening shape, proof placement, nuance block, close shape, and repetition guardrails.
3. Keep the featured-image direction thesis-led and preserve the free/public search note logic.
4. Produce a full `# Blog Architecture` output that the writer can execute without inventing the argument again.

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] The copy brief lock is explicit and usable.
- [ ] The section arc is specific to the chosen family.
- [ ] The featured-image logic supports the thesis and preserves the free/public sourcing flow.

## Veto Conditions

Reject and redo if ANY are true:
1. The architecture reads like a generic outline instead of an argument plan.
2. The copy brief lock does not materially help the writer.
