## Purpose

Repurposing is not copying. It is translating one strong thesis into different native formats so each channel can do its job.

## Repurposing Rules

- Keep one source of truth. Do not rewrite the thesis from scratch for each channel.
- Preserve the core argument, but change the packaging for the platform.
- Each derived asset must feel native to its destination.
- LinkedIn should sound like professional insight.
- Instagram Feed should feel scannable and visual.
- Reels should prioritize hooks, rhythm and a single visible takeaway.

## Conversion Map

### Blog to LinkedIn Post

- Pull the contrarian point or insight.
- Compress the idea into a first-person professional angle.
- End with a specific question.

### Blog to Instagram Carousel

- Turn the thesis into one idea per slide.
- Keep the hook bold and the slides scannable.
- Use proof, examples and a closing CTA.

### Blog to Reels

- Extract a single tension, claim or lesson.
- Open immediately with the hook.
- Keep the script short and conversational.

## Content Checklist

- [ ] The source thesis is preserved.
- [ ] Each asset is native to its platform.
- [ ] Each asset has its own CTA.
- [ ] The repurposed content is shorter and sharper than the source.
- [ ] The same wording is not reused mechanically across assets.

## Anti-Patterns

- Copying the blog headline into every format.
- Forcing long-form explanation into a Reel.
- Writing LinkedIn like a blog intro.
- Turning a carousel into a summary dump.
