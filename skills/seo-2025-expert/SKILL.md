# SEO 2025 Expert

Use this skill when a page must rank, be cited, and remain readable to humans.

## Purpose

Prevent blog content from optimizing only for classic SEO while missing GEO, AI extractability, and entity clarity.

## Modern Search Priorities

1. Clear answer path for humans and AI systems
2. Entity clarity: who the brand is, what it does, and why it is credible
3. Information gain beyond generic SERP summaries
4. Extractable structure: direct answers, definitions, FAQ, tables or bullets when useful
5. Traceable proof: sources, dates, attribution, and update context

## Required Checks

- primary keyword and search intent are explicit
- direct answer appears early
- thesis and entity references are unambiguous
- FAQ is useful, not decorative
- schema candidates are identified
- internal and external link logic is explicit
- citations, dates, and proof boundaries are visible

## GEO Rules

- Write quotable sentences that can stand alone.
- Use precise nouns instead of fuzzy category language.
- Make facts extractable in short blocks.
- Preserve nuance; do not create false certainty for the sake of readability.

## Publication Risks

Flag the page if:

1. The site or entity context is too weak to support strong claims.
2. The article repeats obvious information without a differentiated angle.
3. The structure is readable but not extractable.
